MCB Website Launch

Sep. 2, 2014

We have had a Facebook page for a little while, and a Twitter account, but now with this standalone website, we will be able to keep both the band members and the community up to date with the latest news from the band, as well as to provide reference materials, schedules, and some recordings of our performances (coming soon!).

We're just getting started, so expect a few bumps along the way. But keep your eyes open for new features on this website in the coming weeks and months.
