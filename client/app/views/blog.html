Heritage Harbor Concert

Posted: May 12, 2015

Our final concert of the season will be at the Heritage Harbor Community in Annapolis on Sunday, May 17 at 3:00 PM. You won't want to miss this farewell concert for director John Wakefield, who has been the impetus for this group, and our director since we started in 1995.

April Concert in Bowie

Posted: Mar. 31, 2015

We've got an exciting new event this year: the Bowie Center for the Performing Arts is sponsoring the Maryland Community Band for a concert in April. Come out to hear us on Sunday, April 19 at 3:00pm for our All-American Salute! This will be a concert of American favorites featuring two outstanding soloists, saxophonist Chris Vadala and soprano Millicent Scarlett.
